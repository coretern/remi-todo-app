Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 03, 2026

Welcome to Remi Todo. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your data across our mobile application.

Hybrid Data Model: Remi Todo defaults to local-only storage, but offers an optional Cloud Sync feature for users who wish to preserve their data across devices.

📂 1. Information Collection and Use

We process two types of data depending on your chosen settings:

  • Local Data (Default): Tasks, History, and Missions are stored on your device using encrypted AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you enable Sync.
  • Cloud Data (Optional): If you enable "Cloud Sync", we collect your Email Address and your Task List to store them securely in Google Firebase Firestore. This allows you to restore your data if you switch devices.
  • Calendar Data (Optional): If you enable Google Calendar integration, the app will sync your tasks to your primary Google Calendar.

🔔 2. Notifications & Permissions

The app requests specific permissions to function correctly:

  • Notifications: Used for mission reminders and task updates. This is triggered locally on your device.
  • Haptic Feedback: Uses the device vibration motor for interaction confirmation and physical feedback.

❌ 3. What We Do NOT Collect or Use

For absolute clarity and your peace of mind, Remi Todo does NOT do the following:

  • No Background Audio: The app does not record or play audio in the background.
  • No Real-time Location: We do not track or store your GPS coordinates or live location.
  • No Alarm Scheduling: As per our latest update, the app does not feature "Smart Alarms" or schedule exact system-level alarms.
  • No Third-Party Advertising: We do not include third-party ads that track your behavior.

🛡️ 3. Data Security and Retention

We use industry-standard security measures:

Cloud data is hosted on Google Firebase with restricted access permissions. We retain your cloud data only as long as your account remains active. You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us.

Note: Local-only data is deleted permanently when you uninstall the app.

🌐 4. Third-Party Services

We utilize the following service providers:

  • Google Firebase: Used for Firestore database storage and optional app analytics.
  • Google Calendar API: Used exclusively for syncing your tasks to your calendar upon request.
  • External Links: The 'About' section may link to GitHub or LinkedIn; we do not control their privacy practices.

👶 5. Children’s Privacy

Our application is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
